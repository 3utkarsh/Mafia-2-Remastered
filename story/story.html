Story

Empire Bay is a city of ambition and danger — a place where loyalties are tested and every choice shapes your fate. Follow a gripping, character-driven narrative as you navigate betrayal, family ties and the cost of power.

The American Dream

Vito Scaletta has started to make a name for himself on the streets of Empire Bay as someone who can be trusted to get a job done. Together with his buddy Joe, he is working to prove himself to the Mob in the hopes of living the good life. The game follows the story of Vito Scaletta, a young Sicilian immigrant caught up in the power struggles of competing crime families in the fictional Empire Bay. Rising through the ranks from a common foot soldier to a "made man", Vito finds that doing favors for the Mob may only get him so far. Set in the fictional Empire Bay during the 1940s and 1950s, the game's story follows Vito Scaletta, a Sicilian immigrant and war veteran, as he becomes caught in a power struggle among the city's crime families while attempting to pay back his father's debts and secure a better lifestyle for himself.
